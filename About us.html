Our story

Our baking business began in february 2016 in a small kitchen at my parents house. what started as a passion for baking fresh bread and scones for family quickly turned into something bigger. the early mornings filled with the smell of butter,flour, and freshly brewed coffe.every batch mixed with care and love giving our customers that homely feeling which then led to our mission : to deliver freshness, quality, and flavour in every bite.

at Nokwanda's baked goods we aim to create fresh baked good made with love and time to work with each dough, using traditional methods ensuring that every baked item comes out crispy,fresh and sweet. Be it for busy parents packing lunchboxes, individuals grabbing a quick snack,our health concious customersand event planners. our kitchen is now bigger but our goal remains the same to be the heart of our community and create warm sweet memories with every bite.

Why we do what we do

we bake because food brings people together and makes them happy.

whether you're here for you daily morning coffee runs or for just a simple sweet treat we at Nokwanda's baked goods are honoured to have you at our table
