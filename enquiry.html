Frequently Asked Questions

1. How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

ALL orders must be booked atleast 7 days in advance. if orders are placed in short notice they are subjected to a 25% rush fee and availability.

2. Do you deliver in Nelspruit?

Yes! Available to make deliveries around Nelspruit to Bushbuckridge for orders above R500.delivery fee calculated by distance,

3. What are your prices for birthday cakes?

Our 1kg cakes start at R350. Final cost depends on size, design, and ingredients.

4. Can you cater for events and weddings?

Yes, we cater for 20 to 500 guests. Cupcakes, dessert tables, and wedding cakes available.
